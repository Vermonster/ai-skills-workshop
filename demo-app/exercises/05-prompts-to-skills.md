# Exercise 5: Convert Prompts into Skills

## Goal
Turn ad-hoc prompts into reusable team assets.

## Task
Take two prompts that worked well in earlier exercises and convert them into skill folders under `.github/skills/<skill-name>/SKILL.md`.

## Skill Must Include
- frontmatter with `name` and `description`
- role definition
- constraints/standards
- expected output artifacts
- quality checks

## Success Criteria
- Another developer can use the skill with minimal context.
- Output quality is more consistent than raw prompting.
